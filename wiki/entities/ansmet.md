# ANSMET

## Profile

ANSMET is an ANN acceleration method that combines near-memory processing with hybrid early termination to reduce unnecessary memory traffic and compute.

## Why It Matters

- Shifts ANN optimization from pure index design toward memory-system and execution-path co-design.
- Complements graph/index innovations with hardware-driven efficiency improvements.
