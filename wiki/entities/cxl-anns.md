# CXL-ANNS

## Profile

CXL-ANNS is a software-hardware co-design for ANN search on CXL-disaggregated memory, focused on making billion-point graphs practical without sacrificing accuracy.

## Why It Matters

- It represents a memory-tier/system-co-design direction beyond classic in-memory or SSD-only ANN serving.
- It emphasizes graph traversal optimization under far-memory latency constraints.
